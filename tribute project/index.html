Queen of melody

Lata Mangeshkar, born on September 28, 1929, in Indore, India, is a legendary playback singer whose voice has graced Indian cinema for decades. Revered as the "Nightingale of India," Lata Mangeshkar's remarkable career spans over seven decades, during which she has recorded songs in numerous languages, including Hindi, Marathi, Bengali, and more.Her melodious and versatile voice has made her an icon in the music industry, and she has received innumerable awards and honors for her contribution to Indian music. Lata Mangeshkar's timeless songs have become an integral part of the cultural fabric of the nation, leaving an indelible mark on the hearts of millions.

Biographies

  • Lata Mangeshkar was born on September 28, 1929, in Indore, India
  • She belongs to the Mangeshkar family, where her father, Dinanath Mangeshkar, was a classical singer and theater actor.
  • Lata Mangeshkar started her musical journey at a young age, learning classical music from her father.
  • Her playback singing career began in 1942 with the Marathi film "Kiti Hasaal.
  • Lata Mangeshkar's versatility is evident in her ability to sing in multiple languages, including Hindi, Marathi, more.
  • She has recorded over 30,000 songs in various languages..
